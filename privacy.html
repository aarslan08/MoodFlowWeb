Privacy

Privacy Policy

At MoodFlow, we believe emotional data is deeply personal. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

Data we collect

  • Only the mood entries, notes, and preferences you voluntarily provide.
  • Where it's stored: Locally on your device. Cloud sync is opt-in and end-to-end encrypted.
  • Third parties: We never sell, share, or trade your personal data. Ever.
  • Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage stats to improve the app. No personal content is included.
  • Your rights: Access, modify, export, or delete your data at any time from Settings.

KVKK Aydınlatma Metni (Turkey)

Under the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698:

  • Your personal data is processed only with your explicit consent.
  • You have the right to learn whether your data is being processed.
  • You may request correction, deletion, or anonymization of your data.
  • Data is processed only for specific, legitimate purposes.
  • Appropriate security measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access.

Questions? Email our Data Protection Officer at privacy@moodflow.app
